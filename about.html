Hello, I’m Jameson Brown. I love design—and web design in particular. Though there are some really cool things that can be done with web technologies today, what I typically find myself drawn to and focus upon is plainness and sweetness.

In December 2016 I graduated from the University of Idaho with a BA in Creative Writing (with an emphasis on poetry). This was something I more or less stumbled into after spending time studying computer science and journalism. Despite the narrow focus my degree title may suggest, large parts of my time were devoted to studying literature and film. Overall, what I actually ended up with was a degree that was 1-part creative writing, 1-part literature, and 1-part film.

And though I began my time at university studying computer science, much of my technical experience has come through my own efforts. Whether it’s HTML & CSS, bits of Python or Bash, or SQL and Regex, my technical and coding experience may be a bit eclectic for a poet. To round this all out, I worked for 6 months with a marketing company in Seattle where I picked up the ins and outs of SEO and email marketing.

In the past few months I’ve moved to Auckland with my partner and have been working in my spare time as a freelance web designer & developer. I recently launched a new site for the West London Penguin Swimming Club and am now looking for my next project.

So, I’m available for new projects!
