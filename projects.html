West London Penguin is one of the oldest swimming clubs in London, having existed in one form or another since 1916. In 2017 the club asked me to refresh their website, which functions as the entry point for prospective members as well as their digital archive. I designed and developed a fully responsive site while also transferring all of their content from a proprietary, closed CMS to WordPress.
